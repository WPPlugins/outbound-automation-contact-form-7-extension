Outbound Automation Contact Form 7 extension captures form submits on your WordPress site and upload them to a dynamic List in Outbound Automation.





== Description ==
Outbound Automation Contact Form 7 extension will capture form submits on your WordPress site and upload them to a dynamic List in Outbound Automation.


This is used with Contact Form 7 and Outbound Automation together.  

Dynamic Lists can be created inside the OA dashboard, or from the WP-CF7 page.
